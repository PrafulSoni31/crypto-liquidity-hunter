## Crypto Liquidity Hunter Bot - Major Bug Fixes & Overhaul

### Critical Bugs Found & Fixed

#### Bug 1: SL Direction Validation Missing (signal_engine.py)
- **Problem:** No explicit check that stop loss was on the correct side of entry price
- For SHORT trades, SL could be BELOW entry (should be ABOVE to protect against upward spikes)
- For LONG trades, SL could be ABOVE entry (should be BELOW to protect against downward spikes)
- **Fix:** Added Gate 8 in signal engine - hard reject signals where SL is on wrong side of entry
- SHORT: reject if SL <= entry | LONG: reject if SL >= entry

#### Bug 2: min_sl_gap_pct Parameter Never Enforced (signal_engine.py)
- **Problem:** Config parameter `signal_execution.min_sl_gap_pct = 1.0` existed but was NEVER read by signal engine
- Signals with SL arbitrarily close to entry were being accepted
- **Fix:** Added `min_sl_gap_pct` parameter to SignalEngine __init__(), enforced in Gate 8 validation
- Rejects any signal where `abs(entry - sl) / entry * 100 < min_sl_gap_pct`

#### Bug 3: Position NOT Closing on Binance When SL Hit (position_monitor.py)
- **Problem:** Most critical bug. When price-based SL/TP detection fired via tracking, `_close_trade_now()` only updated DB - never placed a market close order on Binance
- Result: DB said "SL Hit" but position stayed open on Binance forever
- **Fix:** Added `_place_market_close()` call before `_close_trade_now()` when SL/TP is hit
- Also cancels orphaned orders if close fails
