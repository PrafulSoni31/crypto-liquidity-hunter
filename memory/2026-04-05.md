### 2. Trades Closing in 15-20s Without SL/TP Hit
- **Root cause:** Position monitor reads the CURRENT 1m candle's low/high for SL/TP detection. This candle started BEFORE the entry — its wick may have already touched SL price. Monitor sees the wick, triggers `_place_market_close` (MARKET SELL) immediately at market price (not SL), 18 seconds after entry.
- **Evidence:** 14+ CLOSED_ON_EXCHANGE trades all closing within 16-40s. CHR: entry 12:56:45, close 12:57:03 (18s). Exit price above SL (0.018 vs SL 0.01778) → labeled "closed_on_exchange" not "stop_loss" because TOLERANCE check failed.
- **Fix:** Added entry candle grace period to `_check_trade()` in `position_monitor.py`. Skip SL/TP checks until the entry 1m candle completes (top of next minute). After that, only FRESH candles are checked whose low/high can't pre-exist the entry.
- **Test fix:** Updated `_make_trade()` to use `entry_time = now - 5min` by default so tests bypass the grace period. Added `timedelta` import to test file.
