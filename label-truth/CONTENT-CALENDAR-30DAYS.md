## Content Rotation Pattern

Always alternate between these 4 types:
1. **Single Product Decode** — deep dive into one product
2. **Ranking/Comparison** — multiple products ranked
3. **Exposé/Controversial** — shocking findings, banned ingredients
4. **Practical/Helpful** — "best options at [store]" lists

This keeps the channel varied and hits different search intents.

## Viral Triggers to Include

- US vs Europe ingredient comparisons (always goes viral)
- "Banned in [X] countries" angle
- Kids' food exposés (parents share these massively)
- Before/after of reading labels (behavior change content)
- Responding to brand PR statements
